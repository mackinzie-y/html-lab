Single Origin

Our Single Origin Coffee is sourced from the lush highlands of Ethiopia, the birthplace of coffee. Grown at high altitudes, the beans are nurtured by rich volcanic soil and a unique climate that results in a distinctively bright and aromatic cup. With notes of jasmine, citrus, and a hint of berry sweetness, this coffee captures the essence of Ethiopia’s rich coffee culture. Every sip takes you on a journey to the heart of coffee's origins, offering a complex and refreshing flavor profile that celebrates the craftsmanship of local farmers.
