Costa Rica

Originating from the lush highlands of Costa Rica, this coffee is a true expression of the country's rich volcanic soil and ideal growing conditions. Known for its bright acidity, medium body, and vibrant flavors, Costa Rican coffee offers a cup full of fruity notes, often with hints of apple, berry, and citrus. The carefully cultivated beans are hand-picked to ensure the highest quality, delivering a smooth and well-balanced brew. Whether you enjoy it black or with a touch of milk, Costa Rica coffee brings a taste of Central America's natural beauty and heritage to every sip.
